Experience

Otherwise, I have experience with graphic design and video editing through self-taught learning and hands-on experience. Check out my YouTube Channel to see some of my work in those areas.

Education

I am currently attending Arizona State University in their Graphic Information Technology program as I continue to study Web Development. Prior to attending ASU, I attended Barstow Community College where I have completed my Transfer Associates Degree in Computer Science.

Design Concepts

When it comes to design, my creativity can vary between different styles and approaches, always aiming to create visually appealing and functional solutions. The main thing that I try to focus on is making sure that I have a solid design in mind before I start any projects. With my commissions, I ensure to be as thorough as possible when gathering any information that will assist me in completing the project to the best of my ability.

If its not a commission and its a personal project, I do on occasion get stuck on certain pieces and lose motivation to finish them. Although, I don't let this impact any work that is for someone that is not myself, especially if it's paid. My personal projects are always available for me to come back later, but are usually not my priority.

Technical Skills

I have a strong foundation in graphic design and video editing, which I continue to build upon through self-taught learning and hands-on experience.

  • Video Editing - I have prior experience with DaVinci Resolve as my primary editing software. I have recently picked up Adobe Premiere Pro, and I might just switch over due to the ease of use and the fact that I have access to it through my school.
  • Graphic Design - I have experience with Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator with past projects. However, I primarily use Procreate for custom graphics that I need to make, and Canva when it comes to combining elements with my art and design.
  • Web Development - I am currently learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build responsive and interactive websites. At the moment, I am focusing on understanding the fundamentals and building simple projects.
